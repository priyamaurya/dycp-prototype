Upload your accounts or financial statements

For audited accounts, upload your most recent 2 financial years or your most recent financial year if you do not have both.

If your accounts did not require an audit, upload either your accounts or financial statements for your most recent 2 financial years. If you do not have both years, you can upload your most recent financial year.

Auditing requirements are defined in Part 16 of the Companies Act 2006 (opens in new tab) or an overseas equivalent.

What files you can upload

You can upload most file types. For example, PDF, scans, mobile phone photos, Word, Excel and PowerPoint and ZIP files that are smaller than 25MB.

Give the file a descriptive name like 'audited-accounts-2020-2021.pdf' or 'non-audited-accounts-2020-2021.pdf'.
