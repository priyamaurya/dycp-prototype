Public procurement regulations

Find out about UK public procurement regulations

Legislation that applies in England, Wales and Northern Ireland to new procurements from 24 February 2025:

  • Procurement Act 2023
  • Procurement Act 2023 (Commencement No. 1 and Saving Provision) Regulations 2024
  • Procurement Act 2023 (Commencement No. 2) Regulations 2024
  • Procurement Regulations 2024
  • Procurement (Wales) Regulations 2024
  • Procurement Act 2023 (Commencement No. 3 and Transitional and Saving Provisions) Regulations 2024
  • Procurement Act 2023 (Commencement No. 3 and Transitional and Saving Provisions) (Amendment) Regulations 2024
  • Procurement Act 2023 (Consequential and Other Amendments) Regulations 2025

Legislation that applies in England, Wales and Northern Ireland to procurements that commenced before 24 February 2025:

  • The Public Contracts Regulations 2015
  • The Concession Contracts Regulations 2016
  • The Utilities Contracts Regulations 2016
  • Defence and Security Public Contracts Regulations 2011

Public procurement regulations for Scotland:

  • Public Contracts (Scotland) Regulations 2015
  • Concession Contracts (Scotland) 2016
  • Utilities Contracts (Scotland) Regulations 2016
  • Procurement Reform (Scotland) Act 2014
  • Procurement (Scotland) Regulations 2016
  • Defence and Security Public Contracts Regulations 2011

About Find a Tender

More informaton on the Find a Tender service

From 24 February 2025, both above and below threshold notices about new UK procurements will be published on this service, except below threshold in Scotland. Above threshold notices outside Scotland now cover the full life cycle of contracts, from pipeline to termination.

For procurements that started before that, only above threshold notices are published here, usually over £139,688 including VAT. Information on below threshold contracts over £12,000 including VAT (or £30,000 outside central government) is available from:

  • Contracts Finder for England and other non-devolved territories
  • Public Contracts Scotland for Scotland
  • Sell2Wales for Wales
  • eSourcing NI and eTendersNI for Northern Ireland

Find a Tender replaced Tenders Electronic Daily (TED) in the UK on 31 December 2020.
