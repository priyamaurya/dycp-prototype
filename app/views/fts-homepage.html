About Find a Tender

More informaton on the Find a Tender service

From October 2024 all UK public sector procurements above and below threshold will be published on this service.

Before October 2024 only above threshold, usually over Â£139,688 including VAT, were published.

Find a Tender replaced Tenders Electronic Daily (TED) on 31 December 2020 and includes new notices required by the Procurement Act 2023.

You can find information on contracts over £12,000 including VAT (or £30,000 outside central government) on:

  • Contracts Finder for England and other non-devolved parts of the UK
  • Public Contracts Scotland for Scotland
  • Sell2Wales for Wales
  • eSourcing NI and eTendersNI for Northern Ireland
