Find information on UK public procurements and contracts

Find a Tender is where public sector buyers publish procurement, dynamic market and contract notices that suppliers can search and apply for.

From October 2024 all UK public sector procurements above and below threshold will be published on this service. Before October 2024 only above threshold, usually over £139,688 including VAT, were published.

Find a Tender replaced Tenders Electronic Daily (TED) on 31 December 2020 and includes new notices required by the Procurement Act 2023.

You can find information on contracts over £12,000 including VAT (or £30,000 outside central government) on:

  • Contracts Finder for England and other non-devolved parts of the UK
  • Public Contracts Scotland for Scotland
  • Sell2Wales for Wales
  • eSourcing NI and eTendersNI for Northern Ireland

Public procurement regulations

Find out about the regulations that govern UK public procurement.

Public procurement regulations for England, Wales and Northern Ireland for tenders published after October 2024:

  • Procurement Act 2023
  • The Procurement Regulations 2024

Public procurement regulations for England, Wales and Northern Ireland for tenders published before October 2024:

  • The Public Contracts Regulations 2015
  • The Concession Contracts Regulations 2016
  • The Utilities Contracts Regulations 2016
  • Defence and Security Public Contracts Regulations 2011

Public procurement regulations for Scotland:

  • The Public Contracts (Scotland) Regulations 2015
  • The Concession Contracts (Scotland) 2016
  • The Utilities Contracts (Scotland) Regulations 2016
  • The Procurement Reform (Scotland) Act 2014
  • The Procurement (Scotland) Regulations 2016
  • Defence and Security Public Contracts Regulations 2011
