The financial information you'll need

To participate in public procurement under the Procurement Act you need to upload some financial information.

Depending on which of these you have, you need to upload your:

  • most recent 2 financial years of audited accounts
  • most recent financial year of audited accounts, if you do not have the preceding year
  • accounts or financial statements for your most recent financial year, if your accounts did not require an audit

You'll need to know the financial year end covered by the information you give us.
