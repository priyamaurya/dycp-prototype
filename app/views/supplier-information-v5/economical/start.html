The financial information you'll need

To participate in public procurement under the Procurement Act you need to upload some financial information.

You can upload copies of your company's audited accounts covering the most recent 1 or 2 financial years.

If your accounts did not require an audit, you can upload accounts or financial statements covering your most recent financial year.

You'll need to know the financial year end covered by the information you give us.
