Penalties

This includes HMRC findings for deliberate VAT, tax, duty and excise wrongdoing such as fraud, evasion, abusive practice, failure to notify, defeat in respect of notifiable tax arrangements and failure to cooperate with investigation. Convictions

Court rulings
