The data sharing agreement is a memorandum of understanding (MOU) about data shared on the Find a Tender service (FTS) and the joint responsibilities of:

  • the Cabinet Office
  • contracting authorities taking part in public procurement

The MOU explains the:

  • personal and sensitive data collected on FTS
  • role and responsibilities of the Cabinet Office
  • role and responsibilities of the contracting authority
