Create a consortium

This is where two or more suppliers jointly bid on a procurement. Any number of suppliers can come together to form a consortium.

To create a consortium, you'll need:

  • to give it a unique name
  • an address
  • an email address
  • your most recent share code
  • the most recent share codes for the other suppliers
Any supplier you want to add to the consortium must have completed their supplier information and created a share code.

When you've added all suppliers, you'll create a share code for your combined supplier information.
