Create a consortium

A consortium is an unlimited number of two or more suppliers coming together to submit a joint bid.

You'd usually form a consortium when individual suppliers:

  • are not able to meet all the relevant requirements of the tender
  • take the view that they could collectively put in a bid

As the lead organisation creating the consortium, you'll need:

  • a consortium name
  • your most recent share code
  • the most recent share codes for all the other suppliers

If any supplier joining the consortium does not have a share code, they'll first need to complete their supplier information and create one.

When you've added all suppliers, you'll create a share code for your combined supplier information.
