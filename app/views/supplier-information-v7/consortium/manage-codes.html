Share your consortium information

To share the combined supplier information of the consortium with buyers you need to create a share code and file, and declare that all the information is correct.

When you submit a bid for a procurement, the contracting authority will let you know how to give them the share code or file.

If any of the supplier information for any organisation in the consortium changes, you can update its share code on the consortium details page and then create a new combined share code and file to share the new information.
