Register your details as a buyer or supplier of UK public procurements

This service is for both procurement professionals and suppliers to the UK's public and utilities sectors.

Use the service if you are:

  • a contracting authority who publishes notices to buy or supply goods, works, services and utilities, or both
  • an organisation or sole trader who searches for tenders and submits bids to supply goods, works, services and utilities

Suppliers should enter a Companies House number, or an identifier from the list:

  • Charity Commission for England, Wales and Northern Ireland or Scottish Regulator
  • Mutuals Public Register
  • Guernsey Registry
  • Jersey Financial Services Commission Registry
  • Isle of Man Companies Registry
  • NHS Organisation Data Service (ODS)
  • UK Register of Learning Providers (UKPRN number)
  • VAT number
It should take about 5 minutes to register.

You will need to create a GOV.UK One Login or sign in.

If your organisation is already registered with the service, sign in to your account.
