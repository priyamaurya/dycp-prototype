Declaration

Data protection and freedom of information

We are committed to being as open as possible about how we assess and make decisions on Developing Your Creative Practice applications, and how we use information from your application form and other documents you give us.

We handle any personal data you provide in line with UK data protection law. For more information about the personal data that we collect, what we use it for, and your data protection rights, please read our Privacy notice.

As a public organisation we also have to follow the Freedom of Information Act 2000. You must read the Freedom of Information guidance on our website before you sign your application.

How we may use the information in your application

By signing this application form, you acknowledge that we may:

  • use your information to decide whether to offer you a grant
  • share information with individuals or organisations who help us assess and monitor grants
  • hold your data in our database and use it for statistical purposes
  • publish information about grants awarded, including the amount and a summary of the activity
  • carry out checks on the personal and financial information you provide to prevent fraud
